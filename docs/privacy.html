Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains, in plain language, what Tender Reach collects, why, how long we keep it, and how you can ask us to delete it. If anything here is unclear, message us — that's exactly the kind of clarity we're trying to build.

What we collect

If you message our WhatsApp, Messenger, or Instagram assistant

  • Your platform identifier (phone number for WhatsApp; a platform-specific ID for Messenger/Instagram that isn't a phone number and isn't usable outside that platform).
  • Any tender notice content you choose to forward or paste to us.
  • If you join the Founding Supplier Programme: your name, company name, and your answers about your procurement experience — collected through a short conversational interview, never silently.

If you're added to our outreach list (Prospects)

We may add publicly-published business contact information — company name, industry, general location, and contact details the business has already made public for commercial purposes — sourced from directories, Chamber of Commerce listings, LinkedIn, or similar. We do not add any procurement history, notes, or interview answers to this list — that only happens once you've actually responded to us.

What we never collect

  • Location tracking beyond what you tell us directly in conversation.
  • Access to your contact list, message history outside our conversation, or device data.
  • Bulk data scraped from PRAZ's procurement system or any paid tender-listing service. Tender content we work with comes only from what you share with us, or from original public sources.

How long we keep it

  • Tender content you share is kept only as long as needed to deliver your summary and any active reminder — 30 days by default.
  • Founding Supplier Programme responses are kept for as long as the programme is active, used only to guide what we build, and never published or sold, individually or in combination with other suppliers' answers, without your separate explicit consent.

Your right to delete your data

At any time, message us "delete my data" on any platform. This removes your conversation history and, if you completed the Founding Supplier interview, your record from our supplier database — not just a marker that hides it.

What we never do

  • We never sell your information to advertisers, data brokers, or any third party.
  • We never publish a public "score" or rating naming a specific supplier or business without a clear, evidence-based process and a way for that business to respond — and we haven't built this feature yet.
  • We never make access to our core supplier-support features conditional on payment.

Questions

Contact us at tenderreach.info@gmail.com or through any platform listed on our Contact page.
